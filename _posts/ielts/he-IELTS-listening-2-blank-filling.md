**Date:**

> **November 23** 不缩写月份, 不加后缀 23rd (写多必失)
> **23 November**
> 
> **February 11**
> **21 August**

**Date of birth:** 

> `5.1.2020` **May the first twenty twenty**
> 
> `27.1.1972` the twenty seven of the first Nineteen Seventy-two

**Time:** 

> 12小时，不加 am pm
> 
> 2 to 9 == 8:58
> 8:30 == half past eight
> 
> quarter of the hundred 四分之一
> quarter to night == 8:45
> 
>
